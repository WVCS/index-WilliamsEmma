    • Why Books are Better than Movies

    1. Movies have a time limit and can't be too short or too long, while books can be any length
    2. Books encourage creativity, as you picture the book scenes and characters for yourself. Everyone pictures it differently!
    3. Books help you increase your reading skills and broaden your vocabulary, while movies don't have any comparable benefits. And even if you don't care, your parents are much more likely to let you read for hours than watch movies for hours.
